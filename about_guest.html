Getting to Bislig

By Air
Butuan Airport (Bancasi Airport): 150 km from Bislig; bus or van ride takes 4-5 hours.
Davao City Airport (Francisco Bangoy International Airport): 208 km from Bislig; bus or van ride takes 5-6 hours.

By Land
Buses: Regular service by Bachelor Express and Surigao Bus Company connects Davao City, Butuan City, and other Mindanao areas.

By Sea
